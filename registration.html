Before You Register

ELIGIBILITY CHECKLIST

Make Sure You Qualify

  • Currently enrolled in grades 9–12 at a school in Duval, St. Johns, or Nassau County
  • Able to compete on the day of the event (April 12, 2026 at Atlantic Coast High School)
  • Willing to follow safety protocols and coaching staff instructions
  • Parental consent obtained if you are under 18 years old

Registration Timeline

LEAD TIMES & CONFIRMATION

Standard Registration (2–4 Weeks)

  • Registration closes 2–4 weeks before the event date
  • Confirmation email sent within 48 hours of registration
  • Event details and check-in instructions provided 7 days before
  • Athlete number and station assignments available 24 hours before

Urgent Registration (Same Week)

  • Want to register last-minute? Contact us directly
  • Space permitting, we can often accommodate same-week registrations
  • Email contact@904combine.com or call for availability
  • Confirmation guaranteed within 24–48 hours

Policies

REFUND & RAIN PLAN

Refund Policy

  • Registrations are non-refundable. Your $60 entry fee is processed as a tax-deductible charitable contribution to the Beary Good Sports LLC.
  • Athlete name transfers are allowed up to 72 hours before the event. Contact us to arrange.
  • If the event is cancelled due to severe weather or emergency, full credit toward a rescheduled date will be provided.

Rain Plan

  • Light Rain: Event proceeds as scheduled with all outdoor testing conducted.
  • Heavy Rain / Lightning: 30-minute delay protocol. If conditions persist, certain events may move indoors or be rescheduled.
  • Severe Weather: Full event reschedule within 60 days at no extra charge. New date announced within 24 hours.

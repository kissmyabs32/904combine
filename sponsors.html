Questions?

SPONSOR FAQ

Can I customize a sponsorship package?

Absolutely. These are baseline packages. Contact us to discuss custom sponsorship opportunities tailored to your brand and budget.

When does the event take place?

The 904 Combine takes place in April 12, 2026 at Atlantic Coast High School. Exact dates will be announced shortly. Sponsor packages include full event day exposure.

What's included in booth space?

Booth space includes a table and signage area. Premium booths get prime locations near high-traffic stations. You can display materials, hand out branded items, or host brand activations.

How many sponsors can sign up?

We can only have one Title Sponsor, but we have room for multiple sponsors across all other tiers. Naming rights are exclusive within each tier level.

Is there a payment plan?

We offer flexible payment options for larger sponsorships. Contact us directly to discuss payment schedules that work for your budget.

Can I get a media kit or sponsorship agreement?

Yes. Our team will provide detailed sponsorship agreements, media kits, and all marketing materials you need once you reach out.
